Canisters adds a new Ranged subclass centered around a new ammo type: Canisters!
Some weapons launch canisters, creating an explosion on impact. Other weapons deplete canisters to fire a projectile.

[h2]TLDR[/h2]

- Adds 10 Canister ammo items, each with 2 different behaviours that can be used by weapons
- Adds 22 new weapons spaced throughout progression
- Adds 5 new Canister-based accessories
- Multiplayer is supported
- Currently only supports English, see the Steam Workshop page if you'd like to contribute another translation
